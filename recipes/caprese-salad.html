Description

Caprese Salad is a simple and delicious dish that is a classic of Italian cuisine. As Italian chef Giada De Laurentiis once said, "Caprese Salad is a perfect way to celebrate the flavors of summer, with its combination of fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil."

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves,
  • torn 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato slices and mozzarella slices on a platter, alternating them.
  2. Sprinkle the torn basil leaves over the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar to make a vinaigrette.
  4. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
