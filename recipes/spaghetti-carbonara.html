Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 ounces pancetta or bacon, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Add garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and add the egg mixture to the spaghetti. Toss quickly to combine, making sure the eggs don't scramble.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Top with chopped parsley, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious and comforting spaghetti carbonara!
