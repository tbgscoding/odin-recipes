Odin Recipes

  • Spaghetti Carbonara »

    • Spaghetti carbonara is a delicious and creamy pasta dish that originated in Italy. As Italian chef Mario Batali once said, "spaghetti carbonara is the perfect example of a Roman dish that speaks to Italian food in general: a few ingredients, simple to make, but absolutely delicious when they are combined in the right way."

  • Chicken Parmesan »

    • Chicken Parmesan, or "Pollo alla Parmigiana" in Italian, is a classic Italian-American dish that is loved all around the world. As Italian-American chef Lidia Bastianich once said, "Chicken Parmesan is one of those dishes that is beloved by everyone - it's comfort food at its best."

  • Caprese Salad »

    • Caprese Salad is a simple and delicious dish that is a classic of Italian cuisine. As Italian chef Giada De Laurentiis once said, "Caprese Salad is a perfect way to celebrate the flavors of summer, with its combination of fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil."
